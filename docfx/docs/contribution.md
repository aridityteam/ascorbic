# Contribution

By contributing to this library, you are also helping us on improving our software, and we always appreciate that!

You can always file a new issue report about our library on [GitHub](https://github.com/aridityteam/ascorbic/issues) or on our [Discord Server](https://discord.gg/69HWzqVBDE). We want to hear feedback from you, and feedbacks are always appreciated!
